OUR SERVICES

Full Rehab

Revitalize your residential property with comprehensive construction rehab. We offer end-to-end support.

Kitchen Rehab

Enhance your kitchen's ambiance by updating materials and appliances. Whether it's cabinet refacing or custom installations, discover ways to rejuvenate your kitchen's design.

Bathroom Rehab

Unlock endless possibilities for redesigning your home's bathrooms, whether it's a sleek, low-maintenance shower or a full-scale bathroom transformation.

Room Addition

We specialize in custom room additions, expanding living space for Kansas City homes. Collaborating with your vision and budget, we create tailored living spaces that reflect your style.

Hotel Project Improvement Plans

Hotel owners must adhere to a PIP to align with the franchise's latest design standards. This is crucial as it enhances property value and long-term profitability.

Delinquent Sales Tax Audition

Permits and Code Management

Investment Property Analysis
